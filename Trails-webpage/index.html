Trail Name Jerusalem Village Trail with Jericho Covered Bridge Trail
Trail Length in miles 4.3
Trail DifficultyEasy
About the Trail Accessible all year round and primarily used for hiking and mountain biking. A trail that goes through forested land with rushing brooks. Has a bridge and abandoned settlements from the 18th to 19th century called Jerusalem Village. Good for mountain biking.

Trail Name Switchback Trail
Trail Length in miles 3.8
Trail DifficultyEasy
About the Trail Patapsco State Park near Marriottsville, MD. Moderately human trafficked trail that go through forested area with a rushing river with waterfalls. Accessible throughout the year. Dogs must be kept on leash.

Trail Name Thomas Farm Trail
Trail Length in miles 2.4
Trail DifficultyEasy
About the Trail About 2.4 miles long and located near Araby View, Maryland. The trail is great for hiking and normally takes 1-3 hours. Family and Dog friendly.
